What is a Borderless Founder?

A Borderless Founder isn't necessarily already borderless. They're someone who has started to think internationally — about their business, their structure, their residency, their wealth.

They're not anti-government. They're not conspiracy-minded. They're not running from anything.

They simply believe that in a world where businesses can operate globally, teams can work remotely, and capital can move internationally — there's no reason to structure your entire life as though you're confined to the country you happened to be born in.

They want business, wealth, mobility and freedom. Combined. Not one at the expense of the others.
