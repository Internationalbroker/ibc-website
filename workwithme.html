How it works

Every founder's situation is different. The right structure for you depends on your business type, revenue, residency goals, existing commitments and where you want to end up.

The process starts with understanding your picture completely — before a single recommendation is made.

01

Discovery

We map your current structure, goals, jurisdiction options and timeline. No jargon. No generic advice. Just your situation.

02

Strategy

We design a clear path forward — which jurisdictions, which structures, which specialists you need and in what order.

03

Coordination

We sit in the middle. We coordinate your accountants, lawyers and formation agents so you don't have to manage it all yourself.

Is this right for you?

We work best with founders who are already generating revenue ($200k–$5m+), based in a Western country, and have started seriously thinking about international structuring — but keep running into complexity, conflicting advice or simply not knowing where to start.
